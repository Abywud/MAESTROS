Maestros Rules

  • Participants may register multiple squads, each officially listed.
  • Registration fee: KES 300 per participant.
  • Each gameweek champion earns KES 50 from every participant.
  • In case of tie, highest overall points wins.
  • No transfer limit during the league.
  • Late joiners pay KES 400 registration fee.
  • Fees are non-refundable.
  • Failure to pay three weekly winners leads to disqualification.
  • Only registered teams can participate.
