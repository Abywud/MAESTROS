Maestros Rules

  • Participants are allowed to register multiple squads, provided that each squad is officially registered with the league.
  • A registration fee of KES 300 is required per participant.
  • At the end of each gameweek, the weekly champion shall receive KES 50 from each participant. This payment must be completed before the start of the next gameweek.
  • In the event of a tie in gameweek points, the participant with the highest overall points will be declared the winner.
  • There is no limit to the number of transfers allowed during the league.
  • New participants joining after the league has commenced must pay a registration fee of KES 400.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Failure to remit payment to three consecutive weekly champions will result in disqualification from the league.
  • Only registered teams will be allowed to participate.
