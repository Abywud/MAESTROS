Maestros Rules

  • Participants can register multiple squads, each with the league.
  • Registration fee: KES 300 per participant.
  • Weekly champion receives KES 50 from each participant by next gameweek.
  • Ties in points: winner is highest in overall points.
  • Unlimited transfers allowed.
  • New participants pay KES 400 after start.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable.
  • 3 missed weekly payments = disqualification.
  • Only registered teams participate.
